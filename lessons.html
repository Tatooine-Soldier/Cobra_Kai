Instructors

Our talented young instructors Miguel and Aisha will teach you the ways of Cobra Kai Karate. They will help you progress from weakness to brute strength. They are well experienced fighters, Miguel in fact, is the current All-Valley Karate Champion. You are in good hands here in Cobra Kai Karate unlike Miyagi-Do loser dojo.

Times

Our lessons take place in the Cobra Kai Dojo, Receeda, CA. Due to our recent reopening, we currently have only one class but tend to expand in the near future. Our current class has 20 members. Training takes place Monday(6pm), Wednesday(4.30pm), Thursday(6pm) and Friday(4.30pm) evenings. Sessions usually last for 1 or 1.5 hours. Students must attend at least 3 lessons per week if they want to get the most out of their training.
